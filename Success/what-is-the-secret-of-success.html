What is the Secret of Success?


Category -- > Success



In this competitive world everybody wants to be successful. But have you ever wondered how many become successful? Very few. Why? Why only few people make it to that level?

Let’s analyze it with a short story…

Once a boy went to Socrates and asked,

“What is the Secret of Success?”

Socrates took the boy to a river and asked him to cross the river along with him. When they reached the mid of the river. Socrates pulled the boy by hair and drowned his head inside the river. He held him inside the water. The boy desperately kept trying to come out and take a breath. Ultimately Socrates pulled him out and asked what he was looking desperately for inside the water. The boy said that it was oxygen.

Then Socrates told him that if he had the same amount of desperation and determination for success as he had for oxygen. He would definitely be successful.

Every child in 10th std wants to score 90 plus %, every want to be engineer dream of getting the admission in one of the top Engineering colleges but how many of them really achieve it? Again very few. Why? Anyone can say the reason here because most of them did not study as good as compared to the students who achieved it.

So, the reason behind as to why most of the people don’t get the success they always wanted is that they never put their 100% to make it happen. Secret of success lies in firm determination and how badly do you want it.

“When you want to succeed as bad as you want to Breathe.. You are Definitely going to be successful.”
